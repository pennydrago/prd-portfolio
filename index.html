About

I'm an aspiring front-end developer with a passion for creating websites that are beautiful, user-friendly and accessible.

Background

I am a lifelong learner who is changing careers to become a front-end developer. Coming from the publishing industry, I have a strong understanding of creating clear and consistent content for the target audience. I'm excited to apply this experience and my problem-solving skills, which I developed during my PhD research, to building websites. With a focus on user experience (UX) design and user interface (UI) design underlying my approach, I'm excited to work in the web development space.

Experience

During my current pivot to becoming a front-end developer, I have developed this portfolio website, a company home page and single-page web application.

In my previous role as a freelance editor, I used my clear communication skills, excellent attention to detail and effective planning skills to help clients publish science journal papers and educational texts.
